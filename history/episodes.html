Specific Moments That Mattered

Some history is best told as a single event. A fire that destroys a building overnight. A flood that abandons a neighborhood. A shootout at a train station that triggers a federal expansion of police power. These are the moments where the city's larger history concentrates into one day, one decision, one consequence.
