Kansas City History

Eras
How Kansas City Became Kansas City

Kansas City's history breaks naturally into chapters. The river-landing settlement of the 1820s grew into the trail-outfitting town of mid-century, became the railroad boomtown of the late 1800s, and burst into its great Art Deco moment in the 1920s and 30s before mid-century renewal began undoing parts of what had been built. Each era left its physical mark on the city; you can read the past in the buildings.
