About the Blog

A notebook alongside the reference site.

The blog is the part of Look Up, Kansas City that doesn't sit comfortably in the reference architecture of the rest of the site. The history pages and building pages are written as a kind of encyclopedia — careful, attributed, structured. The blog is where I can write about specific moments, particular walks, books I'm reading, restorations I'm watching, and arguments I'm having with myself about how to think about the city.

Posts will appear when they appear. There is no schedule. Some posts will be a paragraph; others will run long. Some will be photo essays from afternoons I've spent walking with a camera; others will be reactions to news about a specific building or neighborhood. The voice is closer to a notebook than a publication.

The site as a whole is a love letter to Kansas City; the blog is the part of the letter where I'm thinking out loud.
